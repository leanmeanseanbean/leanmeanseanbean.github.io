Pay Calculator

This is not an official calculator. There is normally about a 20$ leeway due to rounding inconsistencies.

Any errors, email tidy.rain9146@fastmail.com

    Instructions:

  • All times are written in 24 hour format eg. 1AM is 0100, 6PM is 1800, midnight is 0000 etc.
  • ALL 'Rostered Sign On Time' and 'Rostered Sign Off Time' MUST be filled out for days rostered on.
  • If you did not work a day - RDO, rostered day off - leave the fields blank.
  • Do not write in an ADO, just leave it blank as if it were an RDO.
  • If you were sick, write 'SICK' in the 'Actual Sign On Time' box. eg.  0600  SICK | 1400 _____
  • Remember to fill out the rostered start and finish times for the sick day!
  • Mark the PH box if that day is a public holiday, even if you arent working.
  • If you have a HOL - public holiday not required, write 'HOL' in any of the sign on boxes for that day.
  • This calculator gives the pay for the fortnight only. IT DOES NOT TAKE INTO ACCOUNT ANY WOBODS OR DRIVER OT BONUS FROM PREVIOUS FORTNIGHTS. You need to add this yourself.
