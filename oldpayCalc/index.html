Pay Calculator

This is not an official calculator. There is normally about a 20$ leeway due to rounding inconsistencies. Or i messed up.

Most errors would be on milage shifts or late shifts that finsh the next day. This is due to lack of data and references. Any errors, or you wish to send me timesheets with milage for testing etc, email tidy.rain9146@fastmail.com

    Instructions:

  • If you have javascript disabled, this will not work. Turn on javascript.
  • All times are written in 24 hour format eg. 1AM is 0100, 6PM is 1800, midnight is 0000 etc.
  • ALL 'Rostered Sign On Time' and 'Rostered Sign Off Time' MUST be filled out for days rostered on.
  • If you did not work a day - RDO, rostered day off, or ADO, adjusted day off - leave the fields blank.
  • If you were sick, write 'SICK' in the 'Actual Sign On Time' box. eg.  0600  SICK | 1400 _____
  • Remember to fill out the rostered start and finish times for the sick day!
  • Mark the PH box if that day is a public holiday, even if you arent working.
  • If you have a HOL - public holiday not required, write 'HOL' in any of the sign on boxes for that day.
  • This calculator gives the pay for the fortnight only. IT DOES NOT TAKE INTO ACCOUNT ANY WOBODS FROM PREVIOUS FORTNIGHTS. You need to add this yourself.
